# Official sources

- [Plugins in Codex](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/20001256-plugins-in-codex/): plugin visibility, installation, plan/rollout/workspace and role differences.
- [Using Codex with ChatGPT](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11369540-using-codex-with-chatgpt): Codex surfaces and workspace controls.
- [Admin controls, security, and compliance for plugins and apps](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11509118-admin-controls-security-and-compliance-for-plugins-and-apps): workspace and role controls.

这些链接用于确认产品边界，不替代本机诊断和新 task 的 execution record。产品状态会变化，涉及当前可用性时应重新核对发布日期与页面内容。
